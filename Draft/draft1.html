What this website is for

There is a wide range of classes, and electives that you can pick from, but first you must pick your main classes. These are the classes that you must take in order to graduate high school. The main classes include Math, Science, English, and Social Studies, and Enginearing in 9th grade. Though thesse are not the only classes you can take in 9th grade, they are also electives that you can take. This website is to help you pick your main classes, and electives for 9th grade
